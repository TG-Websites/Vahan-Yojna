3. Loan Terms and Conditions

Interest Rates:

Interest rates are subject to change and depend on various factors including credit score, loan amount, and tenure. Current rates start from 6.5% per annum.

Loan Amount:

We offer financing up to 90% of the vehicle's on-road price for new vehicles and up to 80% for used vehicles.

Repayment Tenure:

Loan tenure ranges from 1 to 7 years, depending on the type of vehicle and loan amount.

  • Vehicle quotation/pro forma invoice
  • Passport-size photographs

All documents must be genuine and verifiable. Submission of false documents may result in immediate rejection and legal action.
