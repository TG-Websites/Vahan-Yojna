Introduction

Vahan Yojana, operated by Shiksh Gravity Foundation, is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our vehicle financing services and website.

By using our services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, date of birth, gender, and contact details
  • Government-issued identification numbers (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Address and residential details
  • Employment information and income details
  • Bank account and financial information
  • Credit history and score information

Technical Information:

  • IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Website usage data and cookies
  • Device information and location data
  • Login credentials and session information

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information through various methods:

  • Directly from you when you fill out application forms
  • From documents you provide during the loan process
  • Through our website using cookies and tracking technologies
  • From third-party sources like credit bureaus and employers
  • From public records and databases
  • Through phone calls and email communications

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Processing loan applications and credit assessments
  • Verifying your identity and preventing fraud
  • Communicating with you about your loan and services
  • Providing customer support and assistance
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improving our services and website functionality
  • Marketing and promotional activities (with your consent)
  • Risk assessment and portfolio management

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

Authorized Third Parties:

  • Credit bureaus and credit information companies
  • Banks and financial institutions for loan processing
  • Insurance companies for vehicle insurance
  • Vehicle dealers and manufacturers
  • Legal and professional advisors

Regulatory Authorities:

  • Reserve Bank of India (RBI)
  • Income Tax Department
  • Law enforcement agencies when legally required
  • Courts and tribunals as per legal orders

5. Data Security and Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection practices
  • Physical security measures for document storage
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Analytics cookies to understand user behavior
  • Marketing cookies for targeted advertising
  • Session cookies for login and security

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the following periods:

  • Loan-related documents: 7 years after loan closure
  • KYC documents: As per RBI guidelines
  • Credit information: As per credit bureau requirements
  • Website data: Up to 2 years unless otherwise required
  • Marketing communications: Until you opt-out

Data is securely disposed of after the retention period expires, unless required by law to be retained longer.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access and obtain copies of your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Right to withdraw consent for processing
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications
  • Right to file complaints with regulatory authorities
  • Right to data portability where technically feasible

9. Third-Party Links and Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services:

  • We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites
  • Please review the privacy policies of external websites
  • Third-party services may have their own data collection practices
  • We recommend exercising caution when sharing personal information

10. Children's Privacy

Protection of minors' privacy:

  • Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years
  • We do not knowingly collect information from minors
  • Parents/guardians should monitor children's online activities
  • Contact us if you believe we have collected a minor's information

11. International Data Transfers

Regarding cross-border data transfers:

  • Your data is primarily processed within India
  • Any international transfers comply with applicable laws
  • Adequate safeguards are implemented for overseas processing
  • Data localization requirements are strictly followed

12. Updates to Privacy Policy

Changes to this Privacy Policy:

  • We may update this policy to reflect changes in practices
  • Material changes will be communicated via email or website notice
  • Continued use of services constitutes acceptance of updates
  • Previous versions will be archived for reference
